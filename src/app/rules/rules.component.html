Welcome to Mario Party '19

Here are the rules of the game:

You and your stack are now immersed in a game of Mario Party. You must traverse the game board (encompassing all of campus), participating in mini-games and missions at every turn. You cannot advance to your next turn until you successfully complete the current mini-game/mission. Stars and coins will be awarded based on your performance in these. Coins can be used to buy items/perks in future games. Whoever has the most stars at the end wins!

The team with the most stars at the end wins! Stars are awarded at the end of each mini-game / puzzle mission. For games involving competition between multiple teams, more stars will be awarded to the winners. There may also be some bonus stars at the end for hidden missions

Coins are also rewarded at the end of mini-games and puzzles, the amount varying based on your performance in the event. Any coins you earn can be used in future activities to purchase perks. These perks may include items to help you out in a mini-game, hints for certain puzzles, etc.

This app is the backbone of the party. Here, you'll find:

The Profile This is your teams profile page. You can track how many stars and coins your team has here.
The Scoreboard Checkout how you're doing relative to the rest of the stack! All teams' stars and coins will be listed here. The Game Go here to progress in the game. Upon unlocking the next turn, you'll be able to roll the dice and move along the board. A clue to the next event location will then be revealed to you.
